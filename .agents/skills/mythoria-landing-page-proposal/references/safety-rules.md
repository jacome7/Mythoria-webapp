# Safety Rules

## Risk Ratings

- `green`: low-risk commercial or celebratory pages.
- `yellow`: child, neurodivergence, grief, memory, school, education-adjacent, health-adjacent, or vulnerable-audience pages.
- `red`: pages that rely on unsafe claims, exploit vulnerability, or cannot be made reviewable without rewriting.

Sensitive pages must set:

- `sensitiveNiche: true`
- `requiresHumanApproval: true`
- `status: "draft"`

## Allowed Claims

Mythoria may help families:

- create personalised stories
- make reading more relevant
- include interests, routines, memories, people, places, or objects
- create stories for reading, listening, sharing, printing, or keeping
- gently introduce a routine, event, transition, or familiar context

For neurodivergent niches, copy may say personalised stories may support:

- familiarisation
- predictability
- shared reading
- emotional connection
- interest-led storytelling

Use `may`, `can`, `pode`, and `pode ajudar` carefully. Never imply a guaranteed outcome.

## Forbidden Claims

Do not say Mythoria:

- treats autism, ADHD, anxiety, grief, sleep problems, or any condition
- diagnoses or evaluates children
- improves behaviour
- replaces therapy, school support, clinical support, or family/professional judgement
- is clinically proven
- guarantees sleep, focus, learning, regulation, behaviour, or emotional outcomes
- should be used by children without adult involvement

Forbidden Portuguese phrases to flag:

- `trata o autismo`
- `cura`
- `diagnostica`
- `clinicamente comprovado`
- `melhora o comportamento`
- `substitui terapia`
- `garante resultados`

## Sensitive Page Disclaimer

Use this note when relevant:

```markdown
A Mythoria não é uma ferramenta médica, terapêutica ou de diagnóstico. É uma forma criativa de transformar interesses, memórias e rotinas em histórias personalizadas para ler, ouvir, partilhar ou guardar.
```

## Research Boundary

Research notes may mention public resources, market substitutes, and broad user needs. They must not turn external therapeutic guidance into Mythoria product claims.
