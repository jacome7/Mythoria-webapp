# Mythoria Voice

Use European Portuguese (`pt-PT`) for user-facing content unless the user asks for another locale.

## Tone

- Direct, clear, warm, and lightly playful.
- Premium but simple; avoid cluttered claims and exaggerated emotion.
- Storytelling-led: memories, routines, little rituals, shared reading, listening, keeping, printing, and gifting.
- Use the phrase `magia da Mythoria` sparingly. It should feel like a small brand cue, not a slogan repeated everywhere.

## Public Copy Preferences

Prefer:

- `Começar a minha história`
- `Criar uma história`
- `Transformar esta memória num livro`
- `Ver exemplos`
- `Guardar esta ideia`

Avoid:

- `Gerar com IA`
- `Criar com inteligência artificial`
- Cold technical explanations before the user understands the value.

## Product Explanation

Explain Mythoria as a service that turns details, memories, interests, photos, people, places, and routines into personalised books that can be read, heard, shared, printed, or kept.

Do not lead with the implementation detail that AI is used. If AI must be mentioned, keep it secondary and plain.

## Style

- Keep headings clear and useful.
- Prefer short paragraphs.
- Use warm concrete examples.
- Avoid fake urgency.
- Avoid fake social proof.
- Avoid public copy that sounds clinical, diagnostic, or therapeutic.
