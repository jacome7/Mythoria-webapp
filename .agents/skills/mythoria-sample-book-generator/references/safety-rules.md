# Safety Rules

## Fictional Samples

Every sample book must be fictional:

- Use fictional names, places, families, and circumstances.
- Do not imply the sample is a real customer story.
- Do not invent testimonials, customer counts, reviews, ratings, press mentions, awards, or clinical proof.
- Do not include real child data, real personal data, or identifying details from user uploads.

## Risk Ratings

- `green`: low-risk celebratory, commercial, adventure, fantasy, romance, sports, hobby, or keepsake themes.
- `yellow`: child, school, neurodivergence, grief, memory, health-adjacent, accessibility-adjacent, or vulnerable-audience contexts.
- `red`: concepts that rely on unsafe claims, exploit vulnerability, or cannot be made public without rewriting.

Yellow or red packs must set:

- `sensitiveNiche: true`
- `requiresHumanApproval: true`
- `status: "draft"`

## Allowed Claims

Mythoria may help people:

- create personalized stories
- turn interests, routines, memories, photos, people, places, or objects into books
- make stories available for reading, listening, sharing, printing, or keeping
- create a fictional example for inspiration
- gently introduce a routine, event, transition, or familiar context

Use careful language such as `may`, `can`, `pode`, and `pode ajudar` for sensitive topics.

## Forbidden Claims

Do not say Mythoria:

- treats autism, ADHD, anxiety, grief, sleep problems, or any condition
- diagnoses or evaluates children
- improves behavior
- replaces therapy, school support, clinical support, or family/professional judgement
- is clinically proven
- guarantees sleep, focus, learning, regulation, behavior, or emotional outcomes
- should be used by children without adult involvement

Forbidden phrases to flag:

- `trata o autismo`
- `cura`
- `diagnostica`
- `clinicamente comprovado`
- `melhora o comportamento`
- `substitui terapia`
- `garante resultados`
- `guarantees results`
- `clinically proven`

## Image Safety

- Show children fully clothed in safe, ordinary settings.
- Prefer supervised family, classroom, bookstore, bedroom reading, or product scenes.
- Avoid intimate framing, bathing, dressing, medical procedures, distress, violence, weapons, and sexualized content.
- Do not use real logos, real school names, real addresses, or visible private documents.
