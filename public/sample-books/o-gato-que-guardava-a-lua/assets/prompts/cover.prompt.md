# Cover Prompt

Create cover art only for a fictional Portuguese children's workshop sample book titled "O Gato que Guardava a Lua". The story is for ages 5-7 and starts from a child's drawing of a family cat.

Use colored pencil drawing, layered colors, visible pencil texture, warm hand-made children's book illustration. Show a gentle grey-and-white cat sitting proudly on a tiled Portuguese balcony at night, guarding a small glowing moon like a ball of yarn. Add children's colored-pencil stars, simple potted basil, a blue cushion, and playful paper fish cutouts.

Portrait book cover. Include the title clearly when possible. No physical book mockup, no visible spine, no 3D object, no logos, no watermark.
