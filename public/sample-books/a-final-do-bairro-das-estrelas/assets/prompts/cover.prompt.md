# Cover Prompt

Create cover art only for a fictional Portuguese children's workshop sample book titled "A Final do Bairro das Estrelas". The story is for ages 8-10 and starts from a child's idea about football, teamwork, and a magical neighborhood tournament.

Use classic European comic album style: crisp ink outlines, flat vibrant colors, clean readable shapes, expressive faces, adventure-comedy tone. Show a mixed group of children in simple unbranded football kits on a small neighborhood pitch at dusk. The ball glows like a tiny star, and chalk constellations on nearby walls show playful tactics.

Portrait book cover. Include the title clearly when possible. No physical book mockup, no visible spine, no 3D object, no club logos, no brand marks, no watermark.
