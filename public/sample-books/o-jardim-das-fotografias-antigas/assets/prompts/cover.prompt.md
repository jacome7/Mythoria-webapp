# Cover Prompt

Create cover art only for a fictional Portuguese children's book titled "O Jardim das Fotografias Antigas". Use watercolor painting with soft brushstrokes, translucent colors, paper texture, and gentle magical realism. Show a family garden with lemon tree, old photos clipped to a line, and a grandmother with two grandchildren discovering glowing photo memories. Portrait cover composition. No physical book mockup, no spine, no 3D object, no logos, no watermark.
