# Cover Prompt

Create cover art only for a fictional Portuguese children's workshop sample book titled "O Clube dos Mapas Impossíveis". The story is for ages 10-12 and starts from kids drawing a map full of secret paths and clues.

Use watercolor painting, soft brushstrokes, translucent colors, paper texture, adventurous but gentle middle-grade illustration. Show three curious children studying a glowing hand-drawn map on the floor of a library workshop room. The map unfolds into tiny paper streets, bridges, keys, and a lighthouse.

Portrait book cover. Include the title clearly when possible. No physical book mockup, no visible spine, no 3D object, no real institution names, no logos, no watermark.
